about

hi.

i am chris.

i am traveling the world with my guitar and my camera to pursue my passion for music, photography, boardsports and adventure. music plays the most important role in my life.

in 2016 i spent a couple of months in byron bay, australia and started singing and writing music.

i just recorded my first album ‘volcano’ on my 3/4 scale travel guitar at hive studios in byron bay and it will be released in july 2016.

i grew up in canada and germany and finished my masters degree in business and media management. during the past years i have been working as a key account manager, sound engineer, deejay, event manager and as a kitesurf instructor.

the story about my first album ‘volcano’

in 2015 two of my friends and i were hiking up a volcano on the island of lombok, indonesia.

we pitched our camp at the crater lake and during the night at 2 am the volcano started to erupt again after many years of being quite. i felt the eruption, woke up, took a look out of my tent and could not believe what i saw.

i spent a few hours watching the eruption and took a picture of probably the most powerful, magical and beautiful event i have ever seen. one of my songs ‘volcano’s heart’ is about this experience.
